# Sucaba
Sucaba is an AI-powered expense intelligence platform for small and medium-sized businesses. The platform analyses .xlsx-formatted credit and debit transactions [ex](https://github.com/OwenLeSann/Sucaba/blob/main/data/dummy_data.xlsx), along with an expense policy [ex](https://github.com/OwenLeSann/Sucaba/blob/main/assets/Brim%20Expense%20Policy.pdf), and presents the data in three easily digestible transaction tabs: Overview, Violations, and Interactive Agent. The platform is designed to help any financial accounts manager, tech-savvy or not, to gain insights into departmental spending against their budget, policy-violating employee transactions (with a severity index), and AI-inferred reports and recommendations. Managers can detect and resolve anomalies and fraud, benchmark employee spending, devise strategies to reduce spending (such as with vendor consolidation), forecast departmental budget use (is Sales on track to overspend this quarter?) and more, all with Sucuba!
